Garden Gnomes

Hello, Damon (黄沐晨)! Protect your cabbage from endless waves of slugs, beetles, and tanky pests.

Controls
• Select a gnome type on the right, then tap empty soil to place it
• Tap an existing gnome to upgrade it and sharpen its attacks
• S key or Salt Storm button = damage all enemies on screen (20s cooldown)
• The waves keep going — each one gets harder and more crowded

Difficulty: ENDLESS — stronger enemies, bigger swarms, and no final wave to beat.
